CONTENT POLICY

www.FreeTV.in is a free service for communication, self-expression and freedom of speech. We believe www.FreeTV.in increases the availability of information, encourages healthy debate, and makes possible new connections between people. It is our belief that censoring this content is contrary to a service that bases itself on freedom of expression. However, in order to uphold these values, we need to curb abuses that threaten our ability to provide this service and the freedom of expression it encourages. As a result, there are some boundaries on the type of content that can be hosted with www.FreeTV.in. The boundaries we've defined are those that both comply with legal requirements and that serve to enhance the service as a whole. If you encounter a blog/post that you believe violates our policies, please report it to us at [FreeTV at outlook . com] with the link of that particular blog/post. We will try to remove that post at earliest.

CONTENT LIMITATIONS

Our content policies play an important role in maintaining a positive experience for you, the users. Please respect these guidelines. From time to time, we may change our content policies so please check back here. Also, please note that when applying the policies below, we may make exceptions based on artistic, educational, documentary, or scientific considerations or where there are other substantial benefits to the public from not taking action on the content.

Adult and Pornographic Content: We do not allow adult content on www.FreeTV.in, including images or videos or text or links that contain nudity or sexual activity. This website has a strict policy against the sharing of pornographic content and any explicitly sexual content where a minor is involved. We also impose strict limitations on the display of nudity.

Intellectual Property: Before sharing any content/material/information/images/links on this website, please be sure you have the right to do so. We ask that you respect copyrights, trademarks, and other legal rights of individuals or corporations. If you are sharing any content/information/images/links from any other website, please make sure you take prior written approval from that particular publisher.

Phishing and Spam: We take the safety of our members seriously and work to prevent attempts to compromise their privacy or security. We also ask/expect that you respect our members by not contacting them for commercial purposes without their con-sent. Currently we do not share or send any emails to the readers of any of the blog posts made available on www.FreeTV.in. We may however change this privacy policy at any time, thus, the readers and visitors are requested to check the latest and updated policy made available on this page.

Malware/Viruses: As an author, do not create posts that transmit viruses, attempt to install software without the reader's consent, or otherwise impact readers with malicious code. This is strictly prohibited on www.FreeTV.in and may result into suspension of your account.

ENFORCEMENT OF CONTENT POLICY

Please report suspected policy violations to us at [FreeTV at outlook . com]. Our team reviews such complaints of policy violation. If any blog post or blogger (a.k.a. blogion) does not violate our policies, we will not take any action against the blog or blog owner. However, if we find that a blogion or a blog post does violate our content policies, we take one or more of the following actions based on the severity of the violation:

  • Delete the blog/post with or without prior intimation.
  • Delete the blog/blog post with or without prior intimation as and when a DMCA complaint is received against it.
  • Put the blog/post behind an interstitial where only the blog author can access the content
  • Delete the offending content, blog/post
  • Disable the Blogion's access to his/her account of www.FreeTV.in
  • Remove the entire blog (and all blog posts) from www.FreeTV.in
  • Report the user to law enforcement
We may also take any of the above actions (or combination of above actions) if we find that a user has created multiple blogs engaging in repeated abusive behaviour. If you have had a blog disabled, do not create a replacement blog that engages in similar activity.
